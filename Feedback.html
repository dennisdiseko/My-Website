Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response to my feedback?

We typically respond to all feedback within 2-3 business days. If you've requested a callback or follow-up, our customer service team will reach out during our business hours.

Can I provide feedback about a specific product?

Absolutely! Please include the product name or order number in your feedback so we can better assist you. Product-specific feedback helps us improve our inventory and descriptions.

Do you offer rewards for feedback?

Yes! Selected feedback submissions may receive a 10% discount code for their next purchase. We also occasionally run feedback surveys with special rewards.

How is my personal information used?

Your information is kept confidential and used solely to improve our services. We never share your details with third parties without your consent. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
